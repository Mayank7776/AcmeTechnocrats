OUR JOURNEY

Buildsworth Solutions Private Limited

Established in June 2017, Buildsworth Solutions is an engineering consultancy firm dedicated to transforming ideas into robust and iconic physical assets.

From our early days in Noida and Ghaziabad, we have focused on providing developers, builders, and corporate houses with high-end, rigorous civil engineering support. We provide architectural blueprints, structural design reviews, MEP layouts, Project Management Consultancy (PMC), and objective property valuations.

Our leadership team brings together decades of site experience and technical acumen, helping client projects meet safety standards, structural durability, and economic budgets.
